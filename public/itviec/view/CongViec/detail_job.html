Mô Tả Công Việc
At Searix, we help companies solve problems through research, design and development using technology. Our analysts work alongside developers to rapidly engineer data-driven solutions, design elegant user experiences, and implement efficient feedback loops. We are sector agnostic, changing the way companies do business in various industries such as in the travel, finance and property sectors, and creating sustainable and mutually beneficial partnerships.
To achieve all that, we hire individuals with exceptional technical skills and the ability and burning desire to tackle complex and ambiguous problems. We value people who have a spirit of smart experimentation and are not afraid to add value from their perspective. Our team is inclusive, international and interdisciplinary.
As a Mid-Level Web Application Full Stack Developer, you will form part of the new growing Vietnamese team that will help with maintaining the company's products, as well as develop new software for proof of concepts. Your main point of contact will be a consultant based in Singapore, but you will also have the support of your other teammates in the upcoming Vietnamese office.

Responsibilities

  • Design, develop, test, deploy, maintain and improve software

  • Manage individual project priorities, deadlines and deliverables on a range of projects from small-sized projects to proof of concept applications

  • Tweak and deploy variations of the company’s lightweight products (based on PHP CodeIgniter)

  • Work with senior designers and developers to convert Sketch files to well-structured and clean HTML / Javascript / SASS code with UX functionalities


Yêu Cầu Công Việc
  • General programming experience in: coding in CI, Laravel and other PHP MVC frameworks, frontend and backend templating engines, database design and SQL languages,
  • Ability to follow, maintain and lead the team on strict coding standards
  • Work independently with minimal instruction
Tại Sao Bạn Sẽ Yêu Thích Làm Việc Tại Đây

We're based in Singapore, and are setting up our first office in Vietnam. This means that you'll get a chance at influencing the culture of your workplace. You'll get the best of all worlds - opportunities to travel to Singapore for work, to work on international projects across multiple industries with the brightest minds, and the familiarity of doing it from your own country.

  • We value disciplined flexibility - as long as you're an ethical team player who's responsible, flexibility will be granted to you.
  • We value iterations and client feedback - we want to learn and evolve.
  • We value competency - our business analysts, sales consultants, designers and developers are extremely experienced, so there's always much to learn from each other.

We also highly value training - each week, we conduct an internal sharing sessions so that the team has a wide breadth of knowledge. Each month, we also conduct an internal master class from a senior to ensure that those who need in-depth skills are given the knowledge and support they need.
